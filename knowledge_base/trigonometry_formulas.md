# Trigonometry Formulas (for Calculus and Algebra)

## Basic Identities
- sin²θ + cos²θ = 1
- 1 + tan²θ = sec²θ
- 1 + cot²θ = csc²θ

## Double Angle
- sin 2θ = 2 sin θ cos θ
- cos 2θ = cos²θ - sin²θ = 2cos²θ - 1 = 1 - 2sin²θ
- tan 2θ = 2tan θ/(1 - tan²θ)

## Half Angle
- sin²(θ/2) = (1 - cos θ)/2
- cos²(θ/2) = (1 + cos θ)/2

## Sum/Difference
- sin(A ± B) = sin A cos B ± cos A sin B
- cos(A ± B) = cos A cos B ∓ sin A sin B
- tan(A ± B) = (tan A ± tan B)/(1 ∓ tan A tan B)

## Product to Sum
- 2sin A cos B = sin(A+B) + sin(A-B)
- 2cos A cos B = cos(A-B) + cos(A+B)
- 2sin A sin B = cos(A-B) - cos(A+B)

## Sum to Product
- sin C + sin D = 2 sin((C+D)/2) cos((C-D)/2)
- cos C + cos D = 2 cos((C+D)/2) cos((C-D)/2)

## Important Values
| θ    | 0° | 30° | 45° | 60° | 90° |
|------|-----|------|------|------|------|
| sinθ | 0 | 1/2 | 1/√2 | √3/2 | 1 |
| cosθ | 1 | √3/2 | 1/√2 | 1/2 | 0 |
| tanθ | 0 | 1/√3 | 1 | √3 | ∞ |

## Common Mistakes
- Sign errors in sum/difference formulas
- Forgetting the ∓ in cos(A±B) (opposite sign)
- Not simplifying before applying identities
