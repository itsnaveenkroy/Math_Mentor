# Algebra: Sequences and Series

## Arithmetic Progression (AP)
- nth term: a_n = a + (n-1)d
- Sum of n terms: S_n = n/2 × (2a + (n-1)d) = n/2 × (first + last)
- Common difference: d = a_{n+1} - a_n

## Geometric Progression (GP)
- nth term: a_n = a × r^(n-1)
- Sum of n terms: S_n = a(r^n - 1)/(r - 1) for r ≠ 1
- Sum to infinity (|r| < 1): S_∞ = a/(1 - r)
- Common ratio: r = a_{n+1}/a_n

## Harmonic Progression (HP)
- A sequence is in HP if reciprocals form an AP
- nth term: 1/a_n where a_n is in AP

## AM-GM-HM Inequality
For positive numbers a, b:
- AM = (a + b)/2
- GM = √(ab)
- HM = 2ab/(a + b)
- AM ≥ GM ≥ HM (equality when a = b)

## Sum Formulas
- Σk (k=1 to n) = n(n+1)/2
- Σk² (k=1 to n) = n(n+1)(2n+1)/6
- Σk³ (k=1 to n) = [n(n+1)/2]²

## Common Mistakes
- Confusing AP and GP formulas
- Using infinite GP sum when |r| ≥ 1
- Off-by-one errors in nth term formula
